Last updated: 06/04/2026

HeadStart Games operates Placer the Game. This Privacy Policy explains what

By playing the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information

This Game is developed and operated by HeadStart Games. If

**2. Age requirement**

The Game is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has

**3. What data we collect and why**

We collect data through three sources. We only collect what is necessary
to operate the Game.

**3.1 Google Play Games sign-in**

When you sign in with Google Play Games, we receive the following
information from Google:

-   Display name (your Google Play Games player name)

-   Player ID (a unique identifier assigned by Google Play Games)

-   Profile photo URL (if set on your Google account)

**Why we collect this:** To identify you as a player, save your game
progress to your account, and display your name on leaderboards and
achievements.

**How long we keep it:** Until you request deletion of your account.

**3.2 Firebase Analytics**

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with
the Game. Firebase automatically collects:

-   App events (e.g. level started, level completed, session started)

-   Session duration and frequency

-   Device model and operating system version

-   Firebase Installation ID (a randomly generated device identifier ---
    not linked to your Google account)

**Why we collect this:** To understand how the Game is played, identify
bugs or performance issues, and improve future updates.

**How long we keep it:** Firebase Analytics retains data for up to 14
months, as per Google\'s standard data retention policy.

Firebase Analytics does not collect your name, email address, or any
directly identifying information. It does not use your data for
advertising purposes.

**3.3 Game data (Firestore)**

We store your game progress and related data in Google Firestore,
Google\'s cloud database. This includes:

-   Game progress (e.g. levels completed, scores, unlocked content)

-   In-game settings or preferences you have saved

**Why we collect this:** To save and sync your game state across
devices.

**How long we keep it:** Until you request deletion of your account and
data.

**4. Who we share your data with**

We do not sell your personal data to anyone. We share data only with the
following third parties, solely to operate the Game:

**Google LLC**

We use Google\'s services for sign-in (Google Play Games), analytics
(Firebase Analytics), and data storage (Firestore). Data transferred to
Google is subject to Google\'s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other
third parties.

**5. How we protect your data**

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

-   All data transmitted between the Game and our servers uses TLS
    encryption (HTTPS).

-   Data stored in Google Firestore is encrypted at rest by default,
    using Google\'s standard encryption.

-   Access to Firestore is restricted --- only authenticated users can
    read or write their own data.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use
industry-standard protections, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

**6. Your rights**

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights over your
personal data:

-   Access --- request a copy of the data we hold about you.

-   Correction --- ask us to correct inaccurate data.

-   Deletion --- request that we delete your account and all associated
    data.

-   Objection --- object to how we are using your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address
in Section 1. We will respond within 30 days. There is no charge for
making a request.

**6.1 How to delete your account and data**

To request deletion of your account and all associated game data:

-   Email us at contactheadstartgames@gmail.com with the subject line \"Data Deletion
    Request\".

-   Include your Google Play Games display name or player ID so we can
    locate your records.

-   We will delete your Firestore game data within 30 days and confirm
    by email.

Please note: Firebase Analytics data is retained by Google for up to 14
months and cannot be individually deleted, as it is aggregated and not
linked to your identity after collection.
